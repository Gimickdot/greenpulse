Global Warming

_____________________________________________________________________

An increasingly pressing issue driven by human activity is global warming, which has come to characterize the modern era.
It began with the unrestrained release of greenhouse gases from industrialization, which started off an unstoppable increasing trend in global temperatures
that has upset ecosystems and upset the delicate balance of our planet's climatic systems.




Planetary Fever Global Warming
necessitates a joint treatment from all of us.



Primary Reasons of Global warming

With fossil fuels like coal, oil, and gas making up more than 75% of greenhouse gas emissions worldwide and almost 90% of carbon dioxide emissions overall, fossil fuels are by far the biggest cause of climate change. Greenhouse gas emissions cover the Earth, trapping heat from the sun.




How can it affect the Environment

Scientists' expected effects, like sea ice loss, ice sheet and glacier melting, sea level rise, and stronger heat waves, are already being felt. Scientists forecast that greenhouse gasses produced by humans will continue to raise global temperatures. There will be a rise and intensification of severe weather damage.




How can you Help to Stop
Global Warming?

One of the easiest and most efficient strategies to combat greenhouse gas-induced climate change is to plant trees. Carbon dioxide (CO2) is one of the main greenhouse gases in the environment that plants absorb as they develop.
