Ditching Anthropocene

The term "anthropocene" refers to the current geological epoch in which human activities have had a significant impact on the Earth's ecosystems and geology. It signifies the profound influence of human beings on the planet, including the alteration of landscapes, climate change, and the extinction of various species.

Climate Change

Climate change refers to the long-term alteration in Earth's climate patterns, including temperature, precipitation, wind patterns, and other factors. It is a complex phenomenon that is primarily caused by human activities, such as the burning of fossil fuels and deforestation, which release greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.

Reforestation Project

Reforestation initiatives involve the deliberate and systematic process of restoring and replenishing forests that have been depleted or destroyed due to various factors such as deforestation, wildfires, or natural disasters.
